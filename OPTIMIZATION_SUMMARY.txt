OPTIMIZATION TECHNIQUE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Instead of reading large files in one go, we now:
  1. Detect reads >512KB
  2. Split into optimal 256KB chunks
  3. Read chunks sequentially
  4. Notify readers after each chunk

Result: Fast allocations + better cache locality + reduced GC pressure
